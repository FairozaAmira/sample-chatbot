# Tender Drafting Guide

When preparing tender submissions:

1. Reference Procurement Policy A to highlight compliance with approval workflows.
2. Provide a risk mitigation section covering supplier dependencies and delivery contingencies.
3. Include a financial summary table describing CAPEX, OPEX, and projected ROI.
4. Capture all clarifications and Q&A in the official email thread and sync to SharePoint daily.

Use this template for consistent tender responses across projects.
